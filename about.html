About Campus QueueFlow

Campus QueueFlow is a real-time queue and wait-time tracking platform developed for the University of Windsor.

Our Goal

Help students save time and reduce waiting by providing accurate, live updates about queues across campus services. Users can view estimated wait times, crowd levels, and queue lengths.

Features

  • View live queue lengths and estimated wait times.
  • Color-coded crowd indicators: Green (Low), Yellow (Moderate), Red (Busy).
  • Dashboard to track all campus service locations.
  • Admin access to update queue status if logged in.
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop.
